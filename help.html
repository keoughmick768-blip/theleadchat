🚀 Getting Started

How do I create an account?
Click the "Sign Up" button on the homepage, enter your email and create a password. You'll then be guided through setting up your business profile and configuring your AI receptionist.
How does the AI know how to respond to my customers?
During setup, you'll provide information about your business, services, and common questions. You can also add Q&A pairs to the knowledge base. The AI uses this information to answer customer inquiries naturally.
Can I customize the AI's personality?
Yes! In the Setup page, you can customize the opening message, conversation goals (answer questions, book appointments, or collect info), and add specific responses for common queries.

📞 Phone & Twilio

Do I need to buy a phone number?
We provide a free trial number for testing. For production use, you can purchase a Twilio phone number directly through our platform, or connect your existing Twilio number.
How does the missed call reply work?
When someone calls and hangs up (missed call), our system automatically sends them an SMS with a link to chat with your AI. If they reply via SMS, the AI responds using your configured knowledge base.
What if a customer wants to talk to a real person?
You can configure your AI to recognize when someone wants human assistance and provide your contact information or a callback request form. You can also set up escalation rules in the settings.

💬 Chat Widget

How do I add the chat widget to my website?
Go to the Chat Widget page in your dashboard, copy the embed code, and paste it into your website's HTML. We provide instructions for WordPress, Squarespace, and custom websites.
Can I customize the widget appearance?
Yes! You can customize colors, position (bottom-left or bottom-right), the welcome message, and more from the Chat Widget settings page.

👤 Account & Billing

How do I reset my password?
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email, and we'll send you a link to reset your password.
How do I cancel my subscription?
Go to Settings > Account and click "Delete Account" at the bottom. Your subscription will be cancelled and data will be removed after 30 days.
