❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How does the missed call auto-reply work?

When a customer calls and you don't answer, our AI automatically sends them a text message within seconds. The message is customized to your business and can answer common questions.

Do I need Twilio?

Yes, we use Twilio for phone numbers and SMS. It's $1/month for a phone number. We'll help you set it up!

Can customers book appointments?

Yes! Connect your calendar (Google, Calendly, or GoHighLevel) and customers can book directly through text.

What happens if I answer the call?

If you answer the call, no auto-reply is sent. Everything works as normal!

Can I customize the auto-reply message?

Yes! Go to Settings and edit your business info. The AI will use that information to generate responses.
